Behind the Brand

Demetria Barlow is the Owner and Chief Design Officer of Fresh Focus, a boutique graphics art agency. Four years ago this agency birthed Restoration Tees which takes all this creativity and applies it to custom apparel printing.

Through these ventures, she fuels her passion for creativity and expression by working with individuals, small businesses, and nonprofit organizations to meet their unique creative communications needs. Whether she’s creating a logo for local start-ups, designing menus for neighborhood restaurants, sketching baby shower invitations for moms-to-be, or printing bulk shirts for a family reunion, Demetria’s clients know they are in for an incredible experience! They can count on partnering with a dedicated professional who understands their project needs and delivers creative designs that reflect their personality and brand.

Demetria is a member of Zeta Phi Beta Sorority and a distinguished graduate of Tennessee State University. Demetria is a single mother to two amazing young women, Ravyn and Carrington.

Demetria is also there for families in their time of bereavement. She is dedicated to designing and printing beautiful obituaries and memorial programs that aim to highlight and reflect upon the beautiful life and legacy of cherished loved ones. It is her goal to perform this service with humility, compassion and the desire to ease the suffering by making this part of the service easier for the bereaved family. She is here to assist you compile the information needed for the memorial program, so please feel free to call and discuss your entire memorial and funeral program needs.
